<p><p>Submarine Active Intercept and Ranging System market consists of two main types: Passive Range and Intercept Sonar Type and Active Range and Intercept Sonar Type. </p><p>Passive Range and Intercept Sonar Type involves detecting and locating submarines by listening to their acoustic signatures without actively transmitting any signals. This market primarily deals with sonar systems capable of passive range finding and intercepting of submarines.</p><p>On the other hand, Active Range and Intercept Sonar Type uses active sonar technology to transmit sound signals and analyze the reflections to detect and track underwater objects, including submarines. This market focuses on sonar systems that actively emit and receive signals for range finding and intercepting submarines.</p></p>
