<p><p>Anti-surge valves are used to protect compressors and other equipment from damage caused by excessive pressure or flow rates. There are three main types of anti-surge valves: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic anti-surge valves are controlled by air pressure, hydraulic anti-surge valves use fluid pressure, and electric anti-surge valves rely on electricity for operation. Each type has its own advantages and applications in different industries, providing solutions for maintaining system stability and efficiency.</p></p>
