<p><p>The Two-Wheeler Position Sensor market includes various types such as Capacitive, Magnetic, Infrared (IR), Force Sensor, and others. Capacitive sensors use changes in capacitance to detect position, while magnetic sensors use magnetic fields for position measurement. Infrared sensors use infrared light to determine position, while force sensors measure position based on applied pressure or force. Other types may include optical sensors, ultrasonic sensors, or Hall effect sensors, each offering unique methods for position sensing in two-wheelers.</p></p>
