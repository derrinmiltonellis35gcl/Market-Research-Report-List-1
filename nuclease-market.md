<p><p>Nuclease market types include DNases and RNases. DNases are enzymes that degrade DNA molecules, resulting in their fragmentation or complete hydrolysis. They find applications in molecular biology and genomics research for DNA purification, DNA sequencing, and gene expression analysis. Both DNases and RNases play essential roles in manipulating nucleic acids and are widely utilized in scientific research and biotechnological processes.</p></p>
