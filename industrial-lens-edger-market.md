<p><p>The industrial lens edger market consists of three main types: manual lens edger, automatic lens edger, and semi-automatic lens edger. Manual lens edgers require human intervention for each step of the lens edging process, providing a high level of control but requiring more time and effort. Automatic lens edgers, on the other hand, automate the entire edging process, decreasing labor requirements and increasing efficiency. Semi-automatic lens edgers combine elements of both, allowing for manual adjustments while automating certain steps. Each type caters to different needs and preferences within the lens edging industry.</p></p>
