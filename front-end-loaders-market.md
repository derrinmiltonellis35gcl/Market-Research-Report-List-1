<p><p>Front-end loaders in the market can be categorized into manual and automatic types. Manual front-end loaders rely on operator control for functions such as lifting, lowering, and dumping materials. On the other hand, automatic front-end loaders use sensors and technology to perform these functions without the need for direct operator input. While manual loaders are more commonly used in smaller operations and offer more control to the operator, automatic loaders are becoming increasingly popular for their efficiency and productivity in larger operations.</p></p>
