<p><strong>The Air Conditioning System Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scroll Type Compressor</li><li>Rotary Type Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning system compressor market includes two main types: scroll type compressors and rotary type compressors. Scroll type compressors use orbiting and fixed scrolls to compress refrigerant gas and are known for their high efficiency and quiet operation. On the other hand, rotary type compressors use a rotating motion to compress gas and are compact and lightweight, making them ideal for smaller air conditioning systems. Both types play a crucial role in the operation of air conditioning systems by compressing the refrigerant gas and facilitating the cooling process.</p></p>
