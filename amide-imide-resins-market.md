<p><p>Amide-imide resins are high-performance polymers known for their excellent thermal stability and mechanical properties. There are two main types of Amide-Imide Resins: linear and branched. Linear Amide-Imide Resins have a straight chain structure, offering high tensile strength and chemical resistance. On the other hand, Branched Amide-Imide Resins have a more complex structure with branching points that enhance flexibility and impact resistance. Both types of resins are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics for their superior properties.</p></p>
