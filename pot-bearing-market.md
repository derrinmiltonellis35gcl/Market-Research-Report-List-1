<p><strong>The Pot Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Sliding Pot Bearing</li><li>Fixed Pot Bearing</li><li>Guided Pot Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pot bearings are commonly classified into three main types: Free Sliding Pot Bearing, Fixed Pot Bearing, and Guided Pot Bearing. </p><p>Free Sliding Pot Bearings allow significant movement in all directions, providing flexibility and low friction for bridges and structures subjected to seismic forces. Fixed Pot Bearings restrict movement to vertical loads, providing stability for bridges with limited horizontal displacement. Guided Pot Bearings allow movement in specific directions, guiding the structure while resisting lateral forces, making them ideal for bridges with complex loading conditions.</p></p>
