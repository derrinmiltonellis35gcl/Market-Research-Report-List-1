<p><p>The Anti-Roll Bar market consists of two main types: Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM) and Aftermarket. OEM refers to anti-roll bars that are installed by car manufacturers during the production of the vehicle. These bars are designed to meet specific vehicle requirements. On the other hand, Aftermarket anti-roll bars are sold separately from the vehicle and can be installed by car owners or mechanics after purchase. They are often used as upgrades to improve the handling and performance of the vehicle.</p></p>
