<p><p>Electronic Assembly Adhesives are used in various applications in the electronics industry. Electrically Conductive Adhesives are designed to provide electrical conductivity between electronic components while also serving as an adhesive. Non-Electrically Conductive Adhesives, on the other hand, offer strong bonding capabilities without conducting electricity. Both types of adhesives play a crucial role in electronic assembly by providing secure connections between components, aiding in heat dissipation, and ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic devices.</p></p>
