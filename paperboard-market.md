<p><p>White Lined Chipboard is a type of paperboard made from recycled paper and cardboard, known for its strength and durability. Virgin Fiber-based board is made from freshly cut wood fiber, offering a high-quality, smooth surface for printing. Coated Unbleached Kraft Board (CUK) is a type of paperboard with a natural brown color and a coating of clay for added strength and printability. These different types of paperboard cater to various packaging and printing needs, providing diverse options for businesses and consumers.</p></p>
