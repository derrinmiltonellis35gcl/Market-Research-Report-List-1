<p><p>Flexible film market can be categorized into various types such as BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene), CPP (Cast Polypropylene), Polyester films, and others. BOPP films are widely used in packaging due to their high tensile strength and clarity. CPP films are known for their heat resistance and moisture barrier properties. Polyester films offer excellent gas barrier and high tensacity. Other types include PVC, HDPE, and LDPE films which cater to different packaging needs in industries such as food, healthcare, and consumer goods.</p></p>
