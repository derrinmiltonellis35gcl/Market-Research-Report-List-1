<p><p>The synthetic membranes market can be categorized into two main types: organic and inorganic membranes. Organic membranes are typically made from polymers such as polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) or polyethersulfone (PES) and are known for their flexibility and high permeability. On the other hand, inorganic membranes are composed of materials like ceramic or stainless steel and are valued for their durability and resistance to harsh chemical environments. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in various industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
