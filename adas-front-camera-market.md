<p><p>The ADAS Front Camera market includes two main types: Monocular and Binocular. Monocular cameras use a single lens and sensor to capture images and depth perception, making them more cost-effective and common in entry-level vehicles. On the other hand, Binocular cameras utilize two lenses and sensors to provide more accurate depth perception and better overall performance, making them ideal for high-end vehicles that require advanced driver assistance features. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing road safety and improving the driving experience.</p></p>
