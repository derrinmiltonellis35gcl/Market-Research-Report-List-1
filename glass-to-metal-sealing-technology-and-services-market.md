<p><p>Glass to metal sealing technology and services refer to the process of creating a hermetic seal between glass and metal materials. There are two types of markets within this industry: glass-metal matching sealing and glass-metal compression sealing. In glass-metal matching sealing, materials with compatible coefficients of thermal expansion are selected to ensure the seal remains intact under varying temperatures. Glass-metal compression sealing, on the other hand, involves applying pressure to the joint to create a tight and secure seal. Both markets cater to different sealing needs in various industries.</p></p>
