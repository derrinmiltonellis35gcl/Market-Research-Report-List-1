<p><p>Architectural insulation materials can be categorized into five main types: rock wool, glass wool, EPS/XPS, PU/PIR, and others. Rock wool and glass wool are made from natural materials and provide good thermal protection. EPS/XPS are lightweight and affordable, while PU/PIR offer higher thermal resistance. The 'others' category includes materials such as cellulose, fiberglass, and natural fibers. Each type of material has its own unique properties and benefits, allowing builders and architects to choose the best option for their specific project requirements.</p></p>
