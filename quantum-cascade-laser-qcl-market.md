<p><p>Quantum Cascade Laser (QCL) technology is segmented into two main market types: Pulsed Quantum Cascade Lasers and Continuous Wave (CW) Quantum Cascade Lasers. Pulsed QCLs are ideal for applications such as spectroscopy and remote sensing, where short pulses of high power are required. On the other hand, CW QCLs are used in applications like gas sensing and medical diagnostics, where a continuous wave of lower power is needed. Both market types offer unique advantages and cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
