They act as a barrier, diverting excess voltage away from the component and safely dissipating it. TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression) Diodes are used to protect electronic circuits from voltage spikes and surges, such as those caused by lightning strikes or electromagnetic interference. Both types of diodes are essential in ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices and are widely used in various industries including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
