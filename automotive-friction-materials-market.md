<p><p>Automotive friction materials market is segmented into various types such as linings, pads, blocks, discs, and others. Linings are used in brake systems to provide friction between the brake shoe and drum or disc. Pads are installed in disc brake systems to create friction between the pad and rotor. Blocks are commonly used in industrial applications for brake systems. Discs are crucial components in disc brake systems. The "others" category may include materials used in clutches, transmission systems, and other automotive applications.</p></p>
