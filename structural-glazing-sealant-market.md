<p><p>Structural glazing sealants are classified based on the type of resin used in their formulation. Resin types include epoxy, polyurethane, acrylic, methyl methacrylate, cyanoacrylate, and others. Epoxy sealants offer strong adhesion and durability, while polyurethane sealants provide flexibility and weather resistance. Acrylic sealants are known for their fast curing times and versatility. Methyl methacrylate sealants are used for their high strength and fast curing properties, while cyanoacrylate sealants offer instant bonding. Other resin types may offer unique features tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
