<p><p>The custom dry ingredients blend market encompasses different types of products, including flakes, flour, bran, and whole form. Flakes refer to ingredients that have been processed into thin, flat pieces, usually achieved through rolling or flattening. Flour refers to finely ground powder made from grains or other food sources. Bran refers to the outer layer of grain that is removed during processing, often used for its fiber content. Whole form refers to ingredients that have not been processed or refined, maintaining their natural state. These different market types offer various options for manufacturers seeking specific attributes for their custom blends.</p></p>
