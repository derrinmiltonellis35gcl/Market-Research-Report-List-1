<p><p>Semiconductor rotary joints come in two main types: Single Channel and Multi Channel. Single Channel rotary joints allow for the transmission of signals or power through one passage, making them ideal for simpler applications. On the other hand, Multi Channel rotary joints can transfer multiple signals or power sources simultaneously through different passages, making them well-suited for more complex systems requiring multiple connections. Both types play a crucial role in enabling seamless communication and power transmission in semiconductor applications.</p></p>
