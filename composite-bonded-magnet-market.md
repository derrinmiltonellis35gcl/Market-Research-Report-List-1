<p><p>Composite bonded magnets can be produced using two main manufacturing methods - injection molding and pressing molding. Injection molding involves injecting a molten mixture of bonded magnetic particles into a mold to form the desired shape. This method is commonly used for complex shapes or large quantities. Pressing molding involves pressing a mixture of bonded magnetic particles into a mold under high pressure to create the final product. This method is suitable for simpler shapes and smaller quantities.</p></p>
