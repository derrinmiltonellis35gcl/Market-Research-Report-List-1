Cathode materials facilitate the movement of positive ions within the battery. Separators act as insulating layers between the anode and cathode, preventing short circuits. Each segment of the market plays a crucial role in the overall battery performance and efficiency. The demand for these materials is growing due to the increasing popularity of electric vehicles and renewable energy storage systems.</p></p>
