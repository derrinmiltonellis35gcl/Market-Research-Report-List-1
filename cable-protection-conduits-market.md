Non-metallic conduits, on the other hand, are usually made of PVC, HDPE, or fiberglass and offer a lightweight and corrosion-resistant option for cable protection. Both types of conduits are essential for safeguarding cables in various industries such as construction, telecommunications, and automotive.</p></p>
