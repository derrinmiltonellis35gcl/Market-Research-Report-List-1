<p><ul><li>Polyvalent</li><li>Monovalent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antivenin refers to a medication used to treat venomous snake bites or scorpion stings. Polyvalent antivenin is created by combining venoms from multiple species to increase its effectiveness against various snake or scorpion bites. On the other hand, monovalent antivenin is specific to a single species' venom and is tailored for treating bites or stings from that particular snake or scorpion only. Both types of antivenin play a crucial role in neutralizing venom and saving lives, but their effectiveness depends on the specific venom encountered.</p></p>
