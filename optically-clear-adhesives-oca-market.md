<p><p>Optically Clear Adhesives (OCA) are used in various industries for bonding transparent materials like glass and plastics. Different types of OCAs include Acrylics, Polyvinyl Acetate, Polyurethane, Silicone, Epoxy, and others. Acrylics are known for their high optical clarity and weather resistance, while Polyvinyl Acetate offers good adhesion to a variety of substrates. Polyurethane OCAs provide flexibility and impact resistance. Silicone OCAs are known for their thermal stability, while Epoxy OCAs offer high strength and chemical resistance. Other OCAs may offer unique properties suited for specific applications.</p></p>
