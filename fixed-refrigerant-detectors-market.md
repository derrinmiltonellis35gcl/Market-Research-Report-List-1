<p><p>Fixed refrigerant detectors market includes two main types: Halide detectors and Electronic detectors. Halide detectors operate based on color changes caused by the reaction between halogens and acids produced from the breakdown of refrigerants. Electronic detectors, on the other hand, use sensors to detect refrigerant gas levels and trigger alarms when a leak is detected. Both types provide crucial safety measures by continuously monitoring refrigerant levels in commercial and industrial settings, ensuring prompt action in case of leaks.</p></p>
