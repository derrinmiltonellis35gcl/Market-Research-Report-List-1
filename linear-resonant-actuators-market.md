<p><p>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs) are categorized into two main types: Motor Type and Non-Motor Type. Motor Type LRAs utilize mechanical motors to generate vibrations, offering high power efficiency and consistent performance for applications like smartphones and wearable devices. Non-Motor Type LRAs, on the other hand, use magnetic or piezoelectric principles to create vibrations, making them ideal for applications requiring precise control and quiet operation, such as haptic feedback in medical devices and automotive interfaces. Both types offer unique benefits for different market needs.</p></p>
