<p><p>Semiconductor wafer electrostatic chucks (ESC) market types include Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. Coulomb type ESCs rely on the attraction between opposite charges to hold wafers in place during processing, while JR type ESCs utilize a combination of Coulomb force and van der Waals force for better control over wafer handling. Both types offer advantages in terms of stability, precision, and efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing processes, catering to different requirements and preferences in the industry.</p></p>
