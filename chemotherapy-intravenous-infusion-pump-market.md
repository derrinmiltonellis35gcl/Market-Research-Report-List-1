<p><p>Chemotherapy intravenous infusion pumps are devices used to deliver chemotherapy medications into a patient's bloodstream. There are three main types of infusion pumps - large volume pump, syringe pump, and elastomeric pump. Large volume pumps are used for continuous infusion of high volumes of medication, syringe pumps are used for precise and controlled delivery of small doses, and elastomeric pumps are disposable pumps that use pressure within a flexible reservoir to infuse medication. Each type of pump has its own advantages and applications in the chemotherapy delivery process.</p></p>
