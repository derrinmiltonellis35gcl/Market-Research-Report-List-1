<p><p>Soluble cocoa fiber can be classified into two main types: organic cocoa fiber and conventional cocoa fiber. Organic cocoa fiber is derived from cocoa beans that have been grown without the use of synthetic fertilizers or pesticides, making it a more natural and environmentally friendly option. Conventional cocoa fiber, on the other hand, is produced from cocoa beans that may have been treated with chemicals during the cultivation process. Both types of soluble cocoa fiber offer health benefits and can be used in various food and beverage applications.</p></p>
