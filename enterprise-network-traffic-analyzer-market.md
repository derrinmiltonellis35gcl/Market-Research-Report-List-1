<p><p>Enterprise Network Traffic Analyzer is a tool used to monitor and analyze network traffic within an organization. There are three main market types for these analyzers: physical, virtual, and cloud. </p><p>Physical analyzers are hardware-based devices that are directly connected to the network infrastructure. Virtual analyzers are software-based solutions that are installed on virtual machines within the organization's network. Cloud analyzers, on the other hand, are hosted in the cloud, allowing for easy scalability and accessibility from anywhere. Despite the differences in their deployment methods, all three types serve the same purpose of capturing and analyzing network traffic data for improving network performance and security.</p></p>
