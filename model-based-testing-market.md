There are two main types of Model Based Testing markets: online test market and offline test market. In the online test market, testing is conducted while the system is running, simulating real-time conditions. In contrast, the offline test market involves testing in an isolated environment without live system connections. Both markets offer their own advantages and are employed based on specific testing requirements.</p></p>
