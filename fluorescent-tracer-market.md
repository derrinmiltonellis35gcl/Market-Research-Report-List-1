<p><p>The fluorescent tracer market can be divided into two main types: powder and liquid. Powder tracers are finely ground particles that can be easily dispersed and are commonly used in applications where a visible fluorescent glow is needed. Liquid tracers, on the other hand, are pre-mixed solutions that are easy to handle and can be directly added to systems for tracking and visualization. Both types of tracers offer unique advantages and are commonly used in various industries for leak detection, flow analysis, and research purposes.</p></p>
