<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld optical attenuators are categorized into two main types based on the type of fiber they are designed to work with: single mode and multimode. Single mode attenuators are designed for use with single mode fiber optic cables, which are used for long-distance communication. Multimode attenuators, on the other hand, are designed for use with multimode fiber optic cables, which are used for shorter distances and lower data rates. Each type of attenuator is specifically tailored to work with its corresponding fiber type, providing precise control over signal strength.</p></p>
