<p><strong>The Forestry Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Felling Equipment</li><li>Extracting Equipment</li><li>On-Site Processing Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forestry equipment market can be categorized into three main types: felling equipment, extracting equipment, and on-site processing equipment. Felling equipment is used for cutting down trees, such as chainsaws and harvesters. Extracting equipment includes machines like skidders and forwarders, which are used to transport the felled trees to a processing site. On-site processing equipment consists of machines like chippers and grinders, which are used to process the trees into usable products such as lumber or chips. Each type of equipment plays a crucial role in the forestry industry.</p></p>
