<p><p>Etch hard mask market is divided into two main types: metal oxide based and carbon based. Metal oxide based hard masks use materials like silicon oxide or titanium oxide to protect the underlying layer during the etching process. On the other hand, carbon based hard masks utilize materials such as diamond-like carbon or amorphous carbon to provide resistance to etching. Both types of hard masks have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
