<p><p>Reprographic paper market types are categorized based on their weight in grams per square meter (gsm). Below 30 gsm paper is often used for lightweight copying and printing applications. The 30-90 gsm range is commonly used for everyday printing needs. The 91-120 gsm category is ideal for double-sided printing. Paper within the 121-180 gsm range is often preferred for marketing materials and presentations. Anything above 180 gsm is considered heavy cardstock suitable for business cards, invitations, and other high-quality prints.</p></p>
