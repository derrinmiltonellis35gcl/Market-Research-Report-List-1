<p><p>The refrigerated ice pack market comprises two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable ice packs are designed for one-time use and are commonly used for short-term cooling needs, such as in lunches, picnics, or injuries. They are generally made from materials that promote efficient cooling and are easy to dispose of after use. Reusable ice packs, on the other hand, are designed to be refrozen and used multiple times. They are often made from more durable and long-lasting materials, making them suitable for longer cooling periods and reducing waste in the long run.</p></p>
