<p><p>Non-contact infrared temperature sensors are available in two main market types: Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensor and SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensor. Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors are mounted on circuit boards through holes, providing stability and durability. On the other hand, SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensors are surface-mount devices that offer compact size and ease of integration. Both types of sensors are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and healthcare for accurate temperature measurements without making physical contact.</p></p>
