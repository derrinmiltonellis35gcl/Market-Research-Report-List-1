<p><strong>The Multiplex Biomarker Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunohistochemistry (IHC) Assay</li><li>Fluorescent In Situ</li><li>Hybridization (FISH) Assay</li><li>Tissue Microarray (TMA) Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multiplex biomarker imaging market encompasses three types of assays. First is the immunohistochemistry (IHC) assay, which uses specific antibodies to detect and visualize proteins in tissues. Second is the fluorescent in situ hybridization (FISH) assay, which uses fluorescently labeled DNA probes to detect and analyze genetic aberrations in cells. Lastly, the tissue microarray (TMA) assay involves the construction of a grid-like array of tiny tissue cores on a single slide, allowing simultaneous analysis of multiple samples. These technologies enable researchers to study multiple biomarkers and their interactions in a single experiment, facilitating better understanding of disease mechanisms and therapy development.</p></p>
