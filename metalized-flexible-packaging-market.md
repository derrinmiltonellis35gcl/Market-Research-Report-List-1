<p><p>Metalized flexible packaging market types include mono extruded structures, which are composed of a single layer of material for basic packaging needs. Insulated structures are designed with multiple layers to provide thermal insulation for food or pharmaceutical products. Laminated structures combine different materials for enhanced barrier properties. Co-extruded structures feature multiple layers with different functionalities, such as barrier protection and sealing properties, offering versatile packaging solutions for various industries. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in the packaging industry.</p></p>
