<p><ul><li>Square Port</li><li>Circular Port</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthomode transducers are used in microwave systems to combine or separate signals based on their polarization. They come in two main market types: Square Port and Circular Port. Square Port OMTs are commonly used in rectangular waveguide systems, while Circular Port OMTs are used in circular waveguide systems. Square Port OMTs offer better performance in terms of loss and isolation, while Circular Port OMTs are preferred for their compact size and ease of installation in circular waveguide systems.</p></p>
