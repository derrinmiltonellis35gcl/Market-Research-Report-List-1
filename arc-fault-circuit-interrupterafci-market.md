<p><p>Arc Fault Circuit Interrupter (AFCI) market consists of different types catering to specific needs. Branch/Feeder AFCIs are designed to protect entire branch circuits. Combination AFCIs (CAFCI) provide protection for both series and parallel arcing. Outlet AFCIs are integrated into electrical outlets for convenience. Other types of AFCIs include portable, plug-in, and extension cord AFCIs. Each type offers varying levels of protection against electrical fires caused by arc faults, ensuring safety in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
