<p><strong>The Inventory Tank Gauging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point level instruments</li><li>Continuous level instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inventory tank gauging systems can be categorized into two main types: point level instruments and continuous level instruments. Point level instruments provide simple on/off level indications at predetermined points in the tank, whereas continuous level instruments offer real-time monitoring and measurement of the liquid level throughout the tank. Point level instruments are generally more cost-effective and suited for basic applications, while continuous level instruments are more advanced and offer accurate, continuous monitoring of tank levels.</p></p>
