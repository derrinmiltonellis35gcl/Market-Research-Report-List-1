<p><strong>The Voltage Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pass Filter</li><li>High Pass Filter</li><li>Band-pass filter</li><li>Band Rejection Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voltage filters are electronic devices used to modify the voltage level of signals in a circuit. There are four main types of voltage filters: low pass filters, high pass filters, band-pass filters, and band rejection filters. </p><p>- A low pass filter allows low-frequency signals to pass through while attenuating high-frequency signals.</p><p>- A high pass filter allows high-frequency signals to pass while attenuating low-frequency signals.</p><p>- A band-pass filter allows a specific range of frequencies, known as the passband, to pass through while attenuating others.</p><p>- A band rejection filter, also known as a notch filter, does the opposite by attenuating a specific range of frequencies while allowing others to pass through.</p></p>
