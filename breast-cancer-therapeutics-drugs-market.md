<p><p>Breast cancer therapeutics drugs market can be divided into various types including mitotic inhibitors, anti-metabolites, hormone receptor drugs, aromatase inhibitors, and HER2 inhibitors. Mitotic inhibitors target cancer cells by disrupting their ability to divide and grow. Anti-metabolites interfere with DNA and RNA production in cancer cells. Hormone receptor drugs block hormones that fuel the growth of certain types of breast cancer. Aromatase inhibitors prevent the production of estrogen. HER2 inhibitors target proteins that promote the growth of cancer cells.</p></p>
