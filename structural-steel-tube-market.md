<p><strong>The Structural Steel Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Tube</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW) Tube</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld (ERW) Tube</li><li>Seamless (SMLS) Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structural Steel Tube market encompasses various types of tubes used in construction and infrastructure projects. Spiral Weld Tube is formed by spiral welding steel strips together, offering high strength and durability. Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW) Tube involves welding two separate submerged arcs to create a strong tube. Electric Resistance Weld (ERW) Tube is made by passing a high-frequency electric current through the metal to create a weld seam. Seamless (SMLS) Tube is produced without any welding, offering a smooth surface and uniform strength.</p></p>
