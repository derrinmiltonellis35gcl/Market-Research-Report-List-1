<p><p>The nerve regeneration market encompasses three main types of products: biomaterials, nerve conduits, and nerve protectors. Biomaterials are substances implanted into the body to support nerve regeneration. Nerve conduits are tubular structures that bridge the gap between damaged nerve ends, allowing nerve fibers to regenerate. Nerve protectors shield injured nerves from further damage and promote repair. These products are vital in treating nerve injuries and disorders, facilitating the regrowth of nerves and restoring normal function, ultimately improving the quality of life for patients.</p></p>
