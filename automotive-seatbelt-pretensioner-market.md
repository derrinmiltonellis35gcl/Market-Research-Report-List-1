<p><strong>The Automotive Seatbelt Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buckle Pretensioner</li><li>Retractor Pretensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive seatbelt pretensioners are designed to tighten the seatbelt in the event of a collision, reducing the slack and providing better protection for the occupants. There are two main types of seatbelt pretensioners: buckle pretensioners and retractor pretensioners. Buckle pretensioners are located near the seatbelt buckle and retract the belt to remove excess slack during a crash. Retractor pretensioners are integrated into the seatbelt retractor mechanism and function by pulling the seatbelt tight upon impact. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing occupant safety during accidents.</p></p>
