<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene therapy technologies market can be classified into Type I and Type II. Type I market involves therapy modalities that aim to correct or replace defective genes to treat genetic disorders. This includes gene augmentation, where a functional gene is introduced into the body to replace a faulty one. On the other hand, Type II market focuses on technologies that modify gene expression without altering the genetic code. This includes gene editing techniques like CRISPR-Cas9, which can selectively modify specific genes.</p></p>
