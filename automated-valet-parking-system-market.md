<p><p>The Automated Valet Parking System (AVPS) market is segmented into two types: Millimeter Wave Radar and Surround View. Millimeter wave radar technology uses high-frequency radio waves to detect objects in the vehicle's surrounding, enabling accurate parking assistance. On the other hand, surround view technology utilizes multiple cameras installed around the vehicle to provide a 360-degree view, facilitating parking maneuvers. Both technologies aim to enhance parking convenience and safety by automating the parking process and reducing the risk of collisions.</p></p>
