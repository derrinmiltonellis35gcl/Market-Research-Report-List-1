<p><p>Electronic package metal heat sinks come in various types, such as Cu/Diamond, Al/SiCp, Al/Sip (Al30Si70), Cu-Mo (Cu30Mo70), Cu-W (Cu20W80), and others. Cu/Diamond heat sinks utilize copper and diamond materials for high thermal conductivity. Al/SiCp heat sinks use aluminum with silicon carbide particles for improved thermal performance. Al/Sip heat sinks have a composition of 30% aluminum and 70% silicon. Cu-Mo heat sinks combine copper and molybdenum for heat dissipation. Cu-W heat sinks use copper-tungsten alloy for enhanced thermal management. Other heat sink variations offer unique thermal conductivity properties.</p></p>
