<p><p>Grain protectants are categorized into different types based on the pests they target. Insect grain protectants are designed to control insects that infest stored grains. Rodent grain protectants are used to prevent damage caused by rodents such as rats and mice. Other types include fungal species grain protectants, which target mold and fungi that can contaminate stored grains, as well as mites and bird grain protectants. Each type of grain protectant is developed to address specific pest problems in grain storage facilities.</p></p>
