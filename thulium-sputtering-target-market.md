<p><p>Thulium sputtering targets are used in various industries for thin film deposition processes. The two main types of Thulium sputtering targets are rotary type and non-rotatable type. Rotary type targets are designed to be rotated during the sputtering process, ensuring uniform erosion and longer target life. Non-rotatable targets are stationary during sputtering and are generally used for simpler sputtering applications where rotational movement is not required. Both types cater to different market demands based on the specific sputtering requirements of each industry.</p></p>
