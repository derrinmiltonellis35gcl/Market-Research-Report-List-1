<p><p>Electric vehicle wireless charging market includes various types of technologies such as inductive technology, radiation technology, and others. Inductive technology uses electromagnetic fields to transfer energy between the charging pad and the vehicle. Radiation technology uses electromagnetic waves to wirelessly charge the vehicle. Other technologies in the market may include magnetic resonance and capacitive coupling. These technologies aim to provide efficient and convenient ways to charge electric vehicles without the need for physical connection between the charging station and the vehicle.</p></p>
