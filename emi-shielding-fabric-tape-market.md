<p><p>EMI Shielding Fabric Tape is available in two main types: Single Sided and Double Sided. Single Sided EMI Shielding Fabric Tape has conductive adhesive on one side, providing electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding for applications where one side needs protection. Double Sided EMI Shielding Fabric Tape has conductive adhesive on both sides, offering complete EMI shielding for applications where both sides need protection. Both types of tapes are commonly used in electronic devices, medical equipment, aerospace, and automotive industries to prevent EMI interference.</p></p>
