<p><p>Tin-free self-polishing antifouling coatings are divided into two main types: copper type and copper-free. Copper type coatings contain copper compounds that release slowly over time to prevent marine growth on the hull of boats. Copper-free coatings use alternative biocides to achieve the same antifouling properties without the use of copper. Both types of coatings are designed to be environmentally friendly while effectively protecting boat hulls from fouling organisms.</p></p>
