<p><p>The rear axle market can be categorized into three types based on their functionality: drive axles, dead axles, and lift axles. Drive axles are responsible for transferring power from the engine to the wheels, providing the necessary torque to propel the vehicle. Dead axles do not receive power and are primarily used for support and weight distribution. Lift axles can be raised or lowered to adjust the load-carrying capacity of the vehicle based on the requirements of the road or terrain.</p></p>
