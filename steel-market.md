<p><strong>The Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon Steel</li><li>Alloy Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon steel and alloy steel are two main types of steel found in the market. Carbon steel is made up of iron with a small amount of carbon, typically less than 2%, making it strong and durable. It is widely used in construction, manufacturing, and transportation industries. On the other hand, alloy steel is made by combining carbon steel with other elements such as chromium, nickel, and manganese to enhance its properties like strength, hardness, and corrosion resistance. Alloy steel is often used in structural applications, machinery, and tool making.</p></p>
