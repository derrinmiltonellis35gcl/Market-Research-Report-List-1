<p><p>Electric Instantaneous Water Heater market is divided into two main types: Whole-House and Point-of-Use. Whole-House heaters are designed to provide hot water for an entire house, often installed centrally and capable of servicing multiple hot water applications simultaneously. On the other hand, Point-of-Use heaters are smaller units intended for specific locations in a house, such as under sinks or near showers, providing hot water on demand for individual fixtures. Both types offer energy-efficient and space-saving solutions for heating water instantly.</p></p>
