There are two main types of rotor flowmeters, including glass rotor flowmeters and metal tube rotor flowmeters. Glass rotor flowmeters are typically used for low-viscosity liquids, while metal tube rotor flowmeters are suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Both types work by utilizing a rotating rotor inside a chamber to measure the flow rate of the liquid passing through the meter.</p></p>
