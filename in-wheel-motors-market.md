<p><strong>The In-Wheel Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Type</li><li>Outer Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Wheel Motors market comprises two main types: Inner Rotor Type and Outer Rotor Type. Inner Rotor Type motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator, resulting in a more compact design and higher torque density. On the other hand, Outer Rotor Type motors have the rotor positioned outside the stator, allowing for better heat dissipation and easier maintenance. Both types offer advantages and disadvantages based on specific application requirements, such as size constraints, efficiency, and durability.</p></p>
