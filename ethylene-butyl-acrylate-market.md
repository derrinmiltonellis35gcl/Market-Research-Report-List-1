Ethyl Acrylate, 2-Ethyl Hexyl Acrylate, and Methyl Acrylate are the three main market types of EBA. Each type offers unique properties and benefits depending on the specific application requirements. Ethyl Acrylate provides good weatherability, impact resistance, and adhesion properties. 2-Ethyl Hexyl Acrylate offers excellent flexibility and adhesion to various substrates. Methyl Acrylate is known for its high reactivity and compatibility with other polymers.</p></p>
