On the other hand, high power infrared quartz heaters are built for larger areas and primary heating, with wattages typically exceeding 1000 watts. Both types offer efficient and cost-effective heating solutions, with low power models being more suitable for personal use and high power models being ideal for commercial or industrial applications.</p></p>
