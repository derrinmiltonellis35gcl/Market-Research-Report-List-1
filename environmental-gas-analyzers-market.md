<p><p>Environmental gas analyzers are devices used to measure and analyze various gases present in the environment. The market for environmental gas analyzers can be categorized into two types: fixed and portable. Fixed gas analyzers are permanently installed in a specific location, continuously monitoring the gases and providing real-time data. They are commonly used in industrial settings, such as manufacturing plants, to ensure safety and compliance. On the other hand, portable gas analyzers are handheld devices that can be easily moved to different locations for on-site monitoring and analysis. They are often used in field studies, environmental monitoring, and emergency response situations.</p></p>
