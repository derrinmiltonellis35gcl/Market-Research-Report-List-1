<p><p>Solvent-based in-mold coatings are divided into two main types: one-component and two-component coatings. One-component in-mold coatings are ready to use and do not require any mixing before application. On the other hand, two-component in-mold coatings consist of two separate components that need to be mixed together before use. Both types of coatings offer various benefits such as providing excellent adhesion, high gloss finish, and abrasion resistance, making them popular choices for in-mold applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
