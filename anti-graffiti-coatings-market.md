<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Anti-graffiti Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sacrificial Coatings</li><li>Semi-sacrificial Coatings</li><li>Permanent Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-graffiti coatings market consists of three main types: sacrificial coatings, semi-sacrificial coatings, and permanent coatings. Sacrificial coatings provide a sacrificial layer that can be easily removed along with the graffiti, without damaging the underlying surface. Semi-sacrificial coatings offer a blend of sacrificial and permanent properties, providing a balance between protection and durability. Permanent coatings form a long-lasting barrier that prevents graffiti from bonding to the surface, allowing for easier removal. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
