<p><p>Automotive door armrest switches come in two main types: button type and induction type. Button type switches require physical pressure to activate, while induction type switches sense the presence of a hand or object without needing to be physically pushed. Button type switches are more commonly found in older vehicle models, while induction type switches are becoming increasingly popular in modern vehicles due to their convenience and ease of use. Both types serve the same purpose of controlling the opening and closing of doors within the vehicle.</p></p>
