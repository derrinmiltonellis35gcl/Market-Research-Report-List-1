<p><p>3D electro-optical image sensor market types include stereo vision, structured light, time-of-flight, and ultrasound technologies. Stereo vision uses two cameras to capture depth information. Structured light projects patterns onto a surface to measure depth. Time-of-flight calculates distance based on the time it takes for light to travel. Ultrasound sensors use sound waves to determine distance. Each technology offers unique advantages and applications in fields such as robotics, autonomous vehicles, and medical imaging.</p></p>
