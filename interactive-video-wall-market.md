<p><p>Interactive video walls are popular in various industries due to their engaging and immersive capabilities. The three main types of interactive video wall technologies are LCD, LED, and DLP. DLP video walls utilize micro-mirror technology to produce vibrant images with high contrast ratio, making them a popular choice for large-scale displays.</p></p>
