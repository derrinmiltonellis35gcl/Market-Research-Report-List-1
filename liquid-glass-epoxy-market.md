<p><strong>The Liquid Glass Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid glass epoxy market types include solventborne and waterborne options. Solventborne epoxy coatings contain volatile organic compounds that evaporate during the curing process, releasing potentially harmful chemicals into the air. On the other hand, waterborne epoxy coatings use water as a solvent, making them environmentally-friendly and safer to use. Waterborne coatings also offer easy cleanup and low odor compared to solventborne coatings. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
