<p><p>The powered data buoy market can be categorized into solar-powered and battery-powered types. Solar-powered data buoys utilize solar panels to convert sunlight into energy, providing a sustainable and renewable power source. They are cost-effective and have longer operational periods. On the other hand, battery-powered data buoys rely on rechargeable batteries for energy storage and can be deployed in areas with limited sunlight. They offer flexibility and can be easily transported to various locations. Both types play a crucial role in collecting and transmitting valuable data from oceans, lakes, or other bodies of water.</p></p>
