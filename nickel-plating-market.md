<p><ul><li>Electroless Nickel Plating</li><li>Electro Nickel Plating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel Plating Market consists of two main types: Electroless Nickel Plating and Electro Nickel Plating. </p><p>Electroless Nickel Plating involves a chemical reaction to deposit a layer of nickel onto a substrate, without the need for an electric current. This method is used for complex shapes and non-conductive surfaces.</p><p>Electro Nickel Plating, on the other hand, uses an electric current to deposit nickel onto a conductive surface. This method allows for precise control over the plating process and is commonly used in industries such as automotive and electronics.</p></p>
