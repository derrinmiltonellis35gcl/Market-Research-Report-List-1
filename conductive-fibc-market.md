<p><p>Conductive FIBC (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers) are used for transporting materials that are prone to electrostatic discharge. There are two main types in the market: woven and non-woven. Woven conductive FIBCs are made from woven polypropylene fabric, providing strength and durability. Non-woven conductive FIBCs are made from synthetic fibers bonded together, offering a lightweight and cost-effective option. Both types help prevent the buildup of static electricity and protect sensitive materials during transportation and storage.</p></p>
