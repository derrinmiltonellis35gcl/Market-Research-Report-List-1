<p><p>The Digestive Tract Stent Market includes two significant types: Esophageal Stents and Biliary Stents. Esophageal Stents are used to treat obstructions or strictures within the esophagus, allowing the passage of food and liquids. On the other hand, Biliary Stents assist in treating blockages or narrowings in the bile ducts, promoting the smooth flow of bile from the liver to the small intestine.
