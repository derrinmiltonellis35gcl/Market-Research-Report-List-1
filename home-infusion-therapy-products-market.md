<p><p>Home infusion therapy products market types include infusion pumps, intravenous sets, IV cannulas, needleless connectors, and vein imaging devices. Infusion pumps are used to deliver medications or fluids directly into a patient's bloodstream. Intravenous sets are tubing systems that connect the infusion pump to the patient's vein. IV cannulas are flexible tubes inserted into the patient's vein for drug delivery. Needleless connectors are used to create a secure connection between IV tubing and medical devices. Vein imaging devices help healthcare providers locate veins for IV cannulation. These products are vital for safe and effective home infusion therapy.</p></p>
