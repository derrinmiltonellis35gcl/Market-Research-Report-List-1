<p><p>Near Field Communication (NFC) payment technology encompasses three market types. NFC Card Emulation enables a mobile device to act as a contactless payment card, allowing users to make secure transactions by tapping their device on a compatible payment terminal. NFC Reader/Writer allows devices to read information from NFC tags or write information to them for various purposes like access control or transportation services. NFC Peer-to-Peer enables two NFC-enabled devices to communicate and exchange information securely, facilitating features like mobile payments or file sharing between devices.</p></p>
