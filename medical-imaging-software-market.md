<p><p>Medical imaging software market types include tomography, ultrasound imaging, radiographic imaging, and combined modalities. Tomography involves creating cross-sectional images of the body using computed tomography (CT) or magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). Ultrasound imaging uses sound waves to produce images of organs and tissues in real-time. Radiographic imaging, such as X-ray and mammography, captures images using electromagnetic radiation. Combined modalities refer to software that integrates multiple imaging techniques, enabling greater diagnostic accuracy and efficiency. These software applications aid healthcare professionals in accurate diagnoses and treatment planning.</p></p>
