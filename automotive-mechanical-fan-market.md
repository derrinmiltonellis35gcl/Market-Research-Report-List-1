<p><p>Automotive mechanical fans can be categorized into three main types: clutch fans, flex fans, and others. Clutch fans are equipped with a fan clutch that engages and disengages based on engine temperature, providing efficient cooling when needed. Flex fans are made of flexible blades that flatten out at higher speeds to reduce drag and improve fuel efficiency. Other types of fans may include direct-drive fans or electric fans. Each type offers unique features and benefits suited for different automotive applications.</p></p>
