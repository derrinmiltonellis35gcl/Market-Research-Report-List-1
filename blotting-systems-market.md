<p><p>The blotting systems market consists of various types, including gel electrophoresis, blotting systems, imagers, and consumables. Gel electrophoresis involves the separation of molecules based on their size and charge using a gel matrix. Blotting systems transfer the separated molecules onto a solid support, such as a membrane, for further analysis. Imagers are used to visualize and record the results of the transferred molecules. Consumables include reagents, membranes, and other materials used in the blotting process. Collectively, these components form a comprehensive market for blotting systems and related products.</p></p>
