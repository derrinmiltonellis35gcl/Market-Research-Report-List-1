<p><p>Pressure infusion devices are used to quickly and efficiently deliver fluids to patients during medical procedures. There are two main types of pressure infusion devices on the market - manual pressure infusors and automatic pressure infusors. Manual pressure infusors require the user to manually pump air into the device to create pressure and infuse fluids, while automatic pressure infusors use a motorized mechanism to generate pressure and deliver fluids. Both types are essential tools in healthcare settings for ensuring timely and accurate administration of fluids to patients in need.</p></p>
