<p><p>Bathroom floor drains come in various types to cater to different needs. Spring-type floor drains feature a spring mechanism to prevent odors from coming back up. Suction stone floor drains use a stone cover to effectively prevent foul smells. Gravity floor drains rely on gravity to remove water and waste efficiently. Other types of floor drains may include linear drains, tile insert drains, or shower channel drains. Each type offers unique functionality to keep bathrooms clean and odor-free.</p></p>
