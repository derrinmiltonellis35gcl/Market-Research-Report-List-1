<p><p>Disposable infusion sets are used in medical settings to administer fluids or medications to patients through an intravenous route. These sets come in two main types - with filter and without filter. Infusion sets with filters help remove particulate matter and air bubbles from the fluid, ensuring a more efficient and safer delivery. On the other hand, infusion sets without filters may be used when filtration is not necessary or when cost effectiveness is a priority. Both types serve the same purpose of delivering fluids to patients effectively.</p></p>
