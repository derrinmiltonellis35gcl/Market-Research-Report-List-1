<p><p>S-Glass composites are widely used in various markets such as filament winding, injection molding, and pultrusion. Filament winding involves wrapping continuous strands of S-Glass around a mandrel to create high-strength composite products. Injection molding utilizes a mold to inject molten S-Glass material into a specific shape, ideal for creating complex geometries. Pultrusion involves pulling continuous fibers through a resin bath and then shaping them into a specific form, offering excellent strength and durability. Each market type offers unique advantages for different applications of S-Glass composites.</p></p>
