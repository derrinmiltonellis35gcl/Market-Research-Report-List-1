<p><p>Ceramic core aluminum nitride (AlN) heaters come in two main types: flat heaters and cylindrical (tube) heaters. Flat heaters have a flat surface that allows for uniform heating of objects placed on top of them. Cylindrical heaters, on the other hand, are tube-shaped and are suitable for applications where the heating element needs to be wrapped around a particular object. Both types of heaters are commonly used in various industrial and commercial applications for their efficient and reliable heating capabilities.</p></p>
