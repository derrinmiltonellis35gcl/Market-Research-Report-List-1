<p><p>The disposable blood transfusion market consists of three main types: intravenous needles, blood transfusion bottles, and transfusion catheters. Intravenous needles are used to access a patient's vein for the transfusion process. Blood transfusion bottles are containers that hold and deliver blood products to the patient. Transfusion catheters are tubes inserted into a vein to facilitate the transfer of blood components. These disposable products are essential in healthcare settings to ensure safe and effective blood transfusions for patients in need.</p></p>
