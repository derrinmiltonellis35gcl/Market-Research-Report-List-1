<p><p>Automatic Sample Stainers come in two main types - Continuous Feed Stainers and Batch Stainers. Continuous Feed Stainers are designed to process a large volume of samples continuously, making them ideal for high-throughput laboratories. On the other hand, Batch Stainers are suited for smaller batches of samples, allowing for more flexibility and control over the staining process. Both types of stainers offer efficient and reliable staining capabilities, catering to the diverse needs of different laboratories.</p></p>
