<p><ul><li>Lubricated</li><li>Lubricant-free</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low maintenance chains are divided into two main market types: lubricated and lubricant-free. Lubricated chains require periodic maintenance and lubrication to ensure smooth operation and prevent wear and tear. On the other hand, lubricant-free chains are designed to operate without the need for additional lubrication, reducing maintenance time and costs. Both types offer benefits depending on the specific application and requirements. Lubricated chains are more common in heavy-duty industrial applications, while lubricant-free chains are preferred in clean and environmentally sensitive settings.</p></p>
