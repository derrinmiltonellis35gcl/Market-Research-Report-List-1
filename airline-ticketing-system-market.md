<p><p>The airline ticketing system market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. In the cloud-based market, the ticketing system is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. This offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness as the system is managed by a third-party provider. On the other hand, the on-premises market involves installing the ticketing system on local servers within the airline's infrastructure. This offers more control and security but may require higher upfront investment and maintenance costs.</p></p>
