<p><p>The unmanned aerial vehicle landing gear market includes various types to cater to different operational needs. Strut landing gear is a simple and efficient design with a single vertical strut. Rocker landing gear has a more complex design with a rocking motion for shock absorption. Pontoon landing gear consists of floats for water landings. Framed landing gear is a traditional design with a sturdy frame structure. Each type has its advantages and is chosen based on factors such as terrain, mission requirements, and cost.</p></p>
