<p><p>LED encapsulant market types include epoxy and silicone. Epoxy encapsulants are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for applications where mechanical stress is a concern. Silicone encapsulants, on the other hand, offer excellent thermal stability and resistance to yellowing, making them a popular choice for high temperature applications. Both types of encapsulants provide effective protection for LED components, with epoxy offering superior mechanical protection and silicone offering excellent thermal performance.</p></p>
