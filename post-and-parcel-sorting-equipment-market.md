Single Push Tray Sorter relies on a single tray pushed by a series of conveyors for sorting. The "Others" category includes any other innovative sorting equipment not classified in the mentioned types. These equipment types facilitate efficient and accurate sorting of mail and parcels in the logistics industry.</p></p>
