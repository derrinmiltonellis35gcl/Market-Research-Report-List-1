<p><p>The IT storage services market can be broadly classified into two types - local managed storage and remotely managed storage. Local managed storage involves the storage of data on-site, within the organization's premises. It allows for direct control and management of the storage infrastructure. On the other hand, remotely managed storage involves storing data on external servers, managed by a third-party service provider. This allows organizations to outsource their storage needs and rely on the provider's expertise and infrastructure for data management and security.</p></p>
