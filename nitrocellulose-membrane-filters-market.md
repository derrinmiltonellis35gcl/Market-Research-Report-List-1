<p><ul><li>Below 0.45 µm Pore Sizes</li><li>0.45 to 6 µm Pore Sizes</li><li>Above 6 µm Pore Sizes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrocellulose membrane filters are categorized based on their pore sizes into three main types: below 0.45 µm, 0.45 to 6 µm, and above 6 µm. Filters with below 0.45 µm pore sizes are used for fine filtration of very small particles, while filters with 0.45 to 6 µm pore sizes are ideal for general filtration applications. Filters with above 6 µm pore sizes are used for larger particle filtration and are commonly employed in industries where a coarser filtration is required.</p></p>
