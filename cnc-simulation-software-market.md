<p><p>CNC simulation software is available in two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CNC simulation software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It allows users to store data and run simulations online, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of collaboration. On the other hand, on-premises CNC simulation software is installed directly on local servers or computers. It provides companies with greater control and security over their data but requires internal infrastructure for operation and maintenance. Both types have their own benefits and organizations can choose based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
