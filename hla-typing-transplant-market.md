<p><p>HLA Typing Transplant Market involves determining the compatibility of Human Leukocyte Antigen (HLA) between a donor and a recipient for organ transplantation. Non-molecular assays refer to traditional methods, like serological testing, where HLA antigens are identified using antibodies. Molecular assays, on the other hand, use techniques like PCR and DNA sequencing to directly analyze the genetic makeup of HLA genes. Both types of assays have their advantages and limitations, and the market for HLA typing includes companies offering products and services related to these assays to facilitate successful organ transplantations.</p></p>
