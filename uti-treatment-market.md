<p><p>UTI Treatment Market consists of different types of medications, including Quinolones, Aminoglycosides, β-Lactam, Azoles, and Others. Quinolones are antibiotics that target bacteria causing UTIs. Aminoglycosides are also antibiotics but work differently by disrupting protein synthesis in bacteria. β-Lactam antibiotics inhibit cell wall synthesis in bacteria. Azoles are antifungal medications used for fungal UTIs. Other medications in the market include sulfonamides, nitrofurantoin, and fosfomycin. Each type of medication targets different pathogens causing UTIs, providing a variety of treatment options for patients.</p></p>
