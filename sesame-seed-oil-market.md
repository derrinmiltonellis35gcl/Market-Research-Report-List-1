<p><ul><li>White Sesame Oil</li><li>Black Sesame Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sesame seed oil market types include white sesame oil and black sesame oil. White sesame oil is extracted from hulled sesame seeds and has a mild flavor, light color, and high smoke point, making it suitable for cooking. On the other hand, black sesame oil is derived from unhulled sesame seeds and has a stronger, nuttier flavor, darker color, and lower smoke point, making it more suitable for flavoring dishes. Both types of sesame oil are popular for their distinct tastes and nutritional benefits.</p></p>
