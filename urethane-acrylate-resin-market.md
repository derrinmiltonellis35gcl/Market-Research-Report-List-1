<p><p>Urethane acrylate resin is commonly found in two market types - low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity urethane acrylate resins are typically used for applications where a fast cure time is needed, such as inks and coatings. On the other hand, high viscosity urethane acrylate resins are preferred for applications where a thicker and more durable finish is required, such as adhesives and sealants. Both market types offer unique properties and advantages depending on the specific needs of the end user.</p></p>
