<p><strong>The Graphite-based Forging Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Forging Lubricants</li><li>Cold Forging Lubricants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite-based forging lubricants are used in the forging process to reduce friction and wear on metal surfaces, allowing for easier shaping and forming. Hot forging lubricants are designed for use in high-temperature forging operations, where the lubricant must withstand extreme heat. Cold forging lubricants, on the other hand, are used in lower temperature forging processes and are formulated to provide lubrication without overheating. Both types of lubricants are essential in ensuring a successful forging operation.</p></p>
