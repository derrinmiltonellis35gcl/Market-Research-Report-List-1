<p><p>Engineered Quartz (E-Quartz) is divided into two main market types: Phaneromer Quartz and Cryptocrystalline Quartz. Phaneromer Quartz is characterized by large, visible crystals, making it ideal for decorative purposes in countertops and flooring. In contrast, Cryptocrystalline Quartz has smaller, barely visible crystals, making it more suitable for industrial applications such as electronics and glass manufacturing. Both market types cater to different industries and consumer preferences, offering a range of options for various uses.</p></p>
