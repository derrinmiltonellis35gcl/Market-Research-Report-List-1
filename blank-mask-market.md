<p><strong>The Blank Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Reflectance Chrome-film Blank Mask</li><li>Attenuated Phase Shift Blank Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low Reflectance Chrome-film Blank Mask is a type of blank mask used in photolithography with a chrome film coating that minimizes reflectance, reducing unwanted interference during the printing process. Attenuated Phase Shift Blank Mask is another type of blank mask that uses a thin phase-shifting material to create phase shifts in the reflected light, allowing for finer resolution and improved contrast in the final printed pattern. Both types of masks are essential in advanced semiconductor manufacturing for achieving high precision and accuracy in pattern replication.</p></p>
